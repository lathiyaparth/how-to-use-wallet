How to Add Money to Wallet

Method 1:

  1. Step 1: Go to your wallet.
  2. Step 2: Enter the amount you want to add.
  3. Step 3: Click the "Proceed to Add Money" button.
  4. Step 4: Enter your debit card details and click on the "Add Money" button.
  5. Step 5: Check if the addition of money was successful.

Currently, we only provide the option to add money using a debit card. However, we are exploring additional options for adding money. Rest assured, your data is fully secure with Google.
